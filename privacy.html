Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

Flooma Digital is a web design agency based in Australia. We build and maintain websites for small and local businesses. Our website is floomadigital.com.au.

What information we collect

We only collect information you give us directly — typically through our contact form or discovery call booking. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and business details.

We may also collect basic analytics data through Google Analytics — things like which pages were visited, how long people stayed, and where traffic came from. This data is anonymous and never tied to an individual.

Why we collect it

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, discuss your project, and if you become a client, to deliver and manage your website. We don't use it for anything else.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We don't sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. The only exceptions are service providers we use to run our business — like Google Analytics and our hosting provider — and only to the extent necessary to operate our services.

How we store it

Any information you share with us is stored securely. We don't store payment information — all payments are handled by third party processors like Stripe.

How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as we have an active relationship with you, or as long as needed to comply with legal obligations. If you'd like your information deleted, just ask.

Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. We don't use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

Your rights

Under Australian Privacy Law you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request corrections, and ask us to delete it. To do any of these, just get in touch.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your information, reach out at support@floomadigital.com
